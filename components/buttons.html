Buttons

There are three types of buttons used throughout the Hostplus website. Each button type serves a different purpose:

  • Primary Button: The primary button is used only for 'Login' and 'Join' prompts in the navigation bar.
  • Secondary Button: The secondary button is used in most instances where a button is required, aside from 'Login,' 'Join' and 'Next Best Action'.
  • Call to Action Button: Used for 'Next Best Action' and 'Concierge' sections of the website.
